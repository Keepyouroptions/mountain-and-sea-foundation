The Heart of Our Foundation

Who We Are

Mountain and Sea Foundation is a nonprofit organization devoted to gardening, nature-inspired arts and scientific creation, connecting people deeply to the places we depend on, and fostering healthy, resilient communities for today and for tomorrow.

Why Support Us

We are an active foundation doing hands-on charity and education to build local communities to share the love of nature. We focus on measurable work: promote gardening, build community gardens, teach children about plants, arts, and science, and engage other adjacent programs. Our leadership is deeply involved and handle daily operations as volunteers.

Mission and Vision

Mission

Our goal is for everyone to do a little each day to share love for nature.

Vision

We envision a future where our shared efforts deepen people's love of nature, where we call our home.
